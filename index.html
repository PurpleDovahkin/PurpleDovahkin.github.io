Tavi's Story

This page is dedicated to an amazing individual lost to the pandemic of suicide. No matter who or when or where, it is always a tragedy, and whatever can be done to disuade people and stem the flow is worthy of trying. If you are considering or know someone who is please contact 911 or the Suicide Prevention Lifeline or somewhere local Suicide Prevention Lifeline. I promise you will crush people you likely don't intend to.
